# Gemini Constitution: Green Stripe Lawn Care Project

## Mission
Build a £75,000, top 0.01% cinematic landing page for 'Green Stripe Lawn Care'.

## Design Principles (Elite Constraints)
1. **The Architecture**: Premium, high-fidelity experience using Next.js App Router, Tailwind CSS, TypeScript, and shadcn/ui.
2. **The Aesthetic (Apple-Style)**:
   - Deep lush greens and warm earthy tones.
   - Heavy glassmorphism.
   - Subtle depth layers and soft drop-shadows.
   - Extreme whitespace and masterful typography.
   - Scroll-driven, cinematic animations.
3. **Dynamic Assets**: Breathtaking, photorealistic background assets (e.g., a perfectly manicured, dark green golf-course quality lawn outside a luxury Cornish home).
4. **The Copy**: High-converting, hyper-local trust-building copy. Position Chris and Jess as bespoke turf experts. Weave in Padstow and Wadebridge case studies and the 7 local service areas.
5. **UI Integration**: Seamlessly integrate the `ImageComparison` component to showcase "Before & After" transformations.

## The BLAST Framework
- **Blueprint**: Strategic layout tailored to high-end bespoke services.
- **Link**: Connecting hyper-local Cornwall context with compelling narratives.
- **Architect**: Robust underlying structure and component hierarchy.
- **Style**: Pixel-perfect aesthetic application.
- **Trigger**: Engaging micro-interactions and scroll-driven revelations.
