Targeted Seasonal Programmes

Moss & Weed Control

We don't blanket-spray. Every treatment is soil-tested and seasonally calibrated — iron sulphate for moss in autumn, selective herbicides in spring. Particularly effective on the damp, mossy lawns common around Launceston.

The Details

What's Involved

Moss and weeds are symptoms, not causes. That's why we never blanket-spray. Instead, we begin with a soil test to understand why moss or weeds have taken hold — whether it's poor drainage, nutrient deficiency, excessive shade, or soil compaction.

For moss, we apply iron sulphate treatments in autumn when moss is actively growing, followed by scarification to remove the blackened moss. In spring, we use selective herbicides that target broadleaf weeds without harming the grass. Every treatment is calibrated to the specific conditions of your lawn.

This approach is particularly effective on the damp, north-facing, mossy lawns common across the Launceston and North Cornwall area. Our targeted programmes typically achieve 90%+ moss reduction within one season.
