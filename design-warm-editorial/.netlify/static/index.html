02

Our Signature Process

4-Pass Scarifying

Most lawn companies scarify once. We run four passes at graduated depths — removing deep thatch layers that standard equipment can't reach. The result is a root system that breathes, absorbs nutrients, and grows back thicker than ever. Our most requested service across the Bude and Widemouth Bay area.

03

Championship-Grade Seed Blends

Overseeding & Top Dressing

We use the same cultivar blends specified for professional golf greens — fine-leaf perennial ryegrass and creeping red fescue — hand-broadcast and top-dressed with organic loam. A staple of our Padstow and North Cornwall lawn programmes.
